Dan Donnan quit his job in 2022 to spend his time making video games. Some of these games can be found on the main page.

After achieving a First class degree in Computer & Video Games in 2013, Donnan spent 5 years working on
over a dozen LEGO titles
at TT Games. Between 2013 and 2014 he tested handheld and mobile games, before joining the console team at the end of 2014 as a Junior Game Scripter. In 2018, and now a Senior Game Scripter, Donnan left the games industry.

The next 3 and a half years were spent as a Software Developer for Citrus Lime. This included refactoring the codebase to properly modernise and scale their POS system, as well as adding in new features from database right through to the front-end.

Now Donnan develops games with MonoGame and Unity in his preferred language of C#, and occassionally has to dabble in the nightmares that are JavaScript and CSS for things like this very website.

If you want to get in contact, send a DM on Twitter.
